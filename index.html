Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our age calculator

How accurate is this age calculator?

Our age calculator is extremely accurate. It accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and even daylight saving time changes when calculating hours and minutes. The algorithm precisely calculates the difference between your birth date and the current date.

Is my personal data stored when I use this calculator?

No, all calculations are performed directly in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or collect your birth date or any personal information. Your privacy is important to us.

How does the zodiac sign calculation work?

We use the standard Western zodiac system based on tropical astrology. The calculator determines which constellation the sun was in at the time of your birth. The dates for each sign are based on the sun's apparent path through the constellations.

Can I calculate someone else's age?

Absolutely! You can enter any valid date of birth to calculate that person's current age. This is useful for parents tracking children's ages, genealogical research, or any other situation where you need to know someone's exact age.

Why does the calculator show different results than other age calculators?

Some calculators use simplified methods that don't account for varying month lengths or leap years accurately. Our calculator provides precise calculations that match official records. Differences may also occur due to time zone considerations when calculating hours and minutes.
